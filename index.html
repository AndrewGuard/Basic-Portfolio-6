About Me

Casey runs the Child Language Development Lab at Northwestern University. She received her B.A. in Economics and Linguistics in June 2015 from Northwestern University. Her focus has shifted towards mobile development and R, and she is interested in how the ChiLDlab can automate/improve data capture, visualization, and sharing.

As a jazz/latin pianist and drummer, when not in the lab Casey enjoys making music, exploring Chicago's music scene, and learning to DJ.
